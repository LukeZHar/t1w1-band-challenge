Brief Description

How they started: Caleb Shomo started the band in 2012 and was writing songs while he was still appart of a band called Attack Attack! he stated beartooth was just ment to be a mere distraction, He would originally record all vocals and instruments solo in a studio until he was joined by the rest of the band. The original name of the band was called 'Noise', but was switched to Beartooth as "noise" was already being used.


Genre: Metalcore, hard rock, hardcore punk, and pop punk


Controversy: Beartooth have been going pretty good staying away from any controversy, Shomo in the early days of Beartooth would often sing about what was happening to him in his life, which he destribed as being heavy and dark. With his new musics he expressed that he's on a new journey for creating music and in life, trying to take better care of himself and learn how to love himself.


Awards: 2016 - Metal hammer Golden gods awards - Best breakthrough band, Loudwire Music Awards - Best rock album and video for 'Aggressive'

2017 - Loudwire Music Awards - Breakthrough band of the year.

2022 - Heavy Music Awards - Best international artist
